How to Use

  1. Select Real Camera or Spoofed (VirtuCam)
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Click Start Capture to begin recording frames
  4. Hold the phone steady, face the camera naturally
  5. Wait for capture to complete
  6. Click Download ZIP to save frames
  7. Extract ZIP to: kcam/analysis/real/ or kcam/analysis/spoofed/
  8. Repeat for the other capture type
